The Akan Naming System

Hello there! This is a web application that takes your birthday details and calculates the day of the week you were born.

Then depending on your gender, it outputs your Akan Name. Akan Naming System are derived from Ghanian culture. In Ghana, children are given their first name according to the day of the week they were born.
